About Us

Bringing innovation to your healthcare experience

Limited access to affordable healthcare in Nigeria, due to poverty and out of pocket payments, inspired us at CSBN Global Alliance Technologies Ltd to develop the HealthPlus - “State-Led” Health Insurance Scheme. The scheme integrates all healthcare facilities and providers within the states, utilizing innovative technological measures to ensure its operation’s effectiveness, management and sustainability.

Health Insurance is a scheme that protects everyone’s health and gives you access to quality healthcare services, where the insurer agrees to pay for some or all of your medical expenses in exchange for a monthly or yearly premium payment. Many Nigerians lack health insurance, despite its importance in preventing financial hardship from healthcare costs.

With the Nigerian National Health Insurance Agency (NHIA), other Health Insurance and Healthcare management organizations covering barely between 3% - 5% of Nigerians, primarily, employees from government and private organizations, there is therefore the need to have a state-level, comprehensive health insurance program, to reach the majority of the population. This is why we designed the “HealthPlus” Health Insurance Scheme Model, serving as a sub-national initiative, to bridge the coverage gap.

Any state that embraces Health Insurance, demonstrates a commitment to improving public healthcare and health outcomes. HealthPlus “State-Led” Health Insurance scheme, is fundamentally an effective tool for channeling healthcare resources and ensuring their success, focusing on effectiveness and sustainability.

To make a significant difference in the Nigerian Health insurance sector, driving healthcare innovation through technology.

To inspire the relevance of HealthPlus, as a tool for healthcare enhancement, national development and global business.

We believe that with “HealthPlus”, Healthcare insurance solution in Nigeria is now available for all.

Who we are

HealthPlus International Corporate Health Solutions Ltd is a Health Insurance & Healthcare Funding Software Solution Company, with a unique, robust and an all-inclusive “State-Led” health Insurance Scheme design model. Backed by Polymed LLC Russia, a leading Russian network of digital clinics, that pioneered a revolutionary, state of the art, mobile digital diagnostic complex/ clinic, for the delivering of a comprehensive range of medical services, using portable, high quality diagnostic equipments for X-ray, ultrasound, mammogram, ECG, etc with rapid laboratory tests and medical examinations, outside the traditional hospital environment, we are able to deliver convenient and efficient healthcare experience to subscribers. HealthPlus, as part of a holding company, relies on the strength of their subsidiary tech solution for global business networking to provide subscribers with access to wide range of features, benefits, products and services
